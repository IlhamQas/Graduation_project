Shahinas Hamdan

Audiologist and Speech Specialist

A Bachelor's degree from Birzeit University and extensive experience in specialized centers. She has advanced expertise in diagnosing and treating speech disorders and conducting hearing assessments.

She has contributed to international initiatives like “Hearing the Call” and holds certifications in Shadow Teaching, Stuttering to Fluency, and Speech and Language Rehabilitation for the Hearing Impaired. Known for her strong communication skills and adaptability, she excels under pressure and in multidisciplinary environments.
